# 🍱 Mess Expense Tracker

A beautiful, 100% free, and offline-capable Progressive Web App (PWA) to track your monthly mess/hostel food expenses. 

Easily input your monthly mess fee, log when you skip a lunch or dinner, and the app automatically calculates your accurate prorated final bill. Never overpay your mess owner again!

## ✨ Features

- **Real-Time Calculation**: Automatically prorates your monthly charge based on active days and missed meals.
- **Glassmorphism UI**: A gorgeous, modern, and fluid user interface built with HTML & Vanilla CSS.
- **Dark Mode**: Fully supports Dark Theme styling for late-night viewing.
- **History Archiving**: Reset your month and securely archive past tracked receipts to your browser's local memory.
- **Detailed Receipts**: Instantly generates an itemized receipt breaking down your base charges and savings.
- **Export to Image**: Download a high-res PNG image of your receipt summary with one click!
- **Daily Notes**: Leave quick text notes alongside days you bought or skipped a meal (e.g., "ate out with friends").
- **Installable PWA**: Works 100% offline! Install it directly to your mobile home screen via Safari or Chrome.

## 📱 How to Run/Install
1. Clone this repository or download the ZIP.
2. Open the `index.html` file in any modern web browser.
3. **To Use on Mobile**: Host this repository on GitHub Pages or Netlify for free. Open the live link on your phone and click **"Add to Home Screen"**.

## 💾 Data Privacy
Because this app relies zero on backend servers or databases, **your data is 100% safe**. All logic and state history relies purely on your device's `localStorage`. Read/write speeds are instant!
